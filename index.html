About Me

A Look Into My Life

Background

Ed Adeagbo, I was born in Nigeria but I moved to London, UK when I was just 3. Have a decently sized family, but I am the youngest child and the only boy. Lived in London up until the 4th grade when my family then moved to Canada and I have lived here ever since, however I did spend a year in Houston, TX when I was 12 then came back to Canada.

For the first few years I could not stand this province due to the sudden change in weather but I've now grown accustomed to it (I don't even wear a jacket when it's -1 anymore). My family used to travel a lot and I visited a lot of cool places such as:

  • France
  • Spain
  • California (won a free trip to Disneyland)

Hobbies

Ever since I was young I was really into sports, the 2 main sports I played and still actively play are volleyball and basketball. I got into volleyball because my junior high team didn't have enough members for a team (cause we all thought it was a "girls sport") but the coach convinced me and a few others to try out and from there I couldn't stop playing.

I am a HUGE anime and manga fan which eventually led me to Marvel, but animanga will always be my favourite form of media. The first anime I watched was Fairy Tail, but after years and years of consistently watching new animes I think it's safe to name my top 3 (in no specific order):

  • One Piece
  • Attack on Titan
  • Jojo's Bizzare Adventures

*Random note: Spiderman is my favourite MCU hero
